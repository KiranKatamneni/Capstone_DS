About StoryTeller AI

StoryTeller AI combines technology and imagination, letting you explore the stories that live within you. Dive in, create, and let your next adventure begin!

Inspiration

StoryTeller AI was born from a vision to use generative AI to provide a personalized storytelling experience that sparks creativity. With StoryTeller AI, users can become protagonists in their own unique adventures—whether it's a thrilling sci-fi quest, a medieval fantasy, or a nostalgic slice-of-life story capturing everyday moments. Our goal is to empower users to create and experience stories that reflect their imagination and curiosity.

How We Built It

To bring StoryTeller AI to life, we trained OpenAI’s GPT-2 Medium model on a dataset of over 31,000 short stories for children aged 4-16. Using Python for core functionality, HTML/CSS for the front-end, and Flask for backend routing, we created a seamless, interactive platform that makes storytelling accessible and enjoyable.

Key Features

  • Intuitive Prompting: Users can create custom stories by answering a few questions to shape their prompt, with StoryTeller AI crafting an engaging narrative.
  • ‘Surprise Me’ Option: Generates a story without user input, allowing for a fun, random storytelling experience.
  • Story Metrics: Users can view readability and coherence metrics like perplexity and the Flesch-Kincaid index after generating a story.
  • Easy Copying: Save your generated story with a single click, making it easy to revisit or share.

Challenges

Resource Constraints: Limited computational resources restricted us from using larger language models like LLAMA, so we optimized with GPT-2 Medium.

Deployment Strategy: Balancing scalability, accessibility, and cost-efficiency was crucial to find the optimal deployment environment.

Accomplishments

  • Seamless Integration: Achieved robust connectivity across all tech stack components for a well-integrated platform.
  • User-Friendly Design: Our intuitive front-end prioritizes ease of use and visual appeal, allowing users to navigate effortlessly.
  • Hands-On Learning: Gained invaluable experience in software development and tackled real-world challenges with multiple tools and technologies.
